% About AlfaisalX
\section{About AlfaisalX}

\begin{aboutbox}
\textbf{AlfaisalX} (Alfaisal Center for Robotics and Autonomous Agents) is a newly established \textbf{Center of Excellence} at Alfaisal University, dedicated to advancing the frontiers of cognitive robotics, autonomous systems, and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to drive innovation, develop cutting-edge technologies, and cultivate the next generation of AI and robotics leaders in alignment with \textbf{Saudi Vision 2030}.

\vspace{8pt}

\textbf{Alfaisal University}, located in Riyadh, is one of the leading private research universities in the Middle East. The \textbf{College of Engineering} houses state-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities, offering an exceptional environment for groundbreaking research. Our faculty includes internationally recognized experts who have published extensively in top-tier venues.

\vspace{8pt}

\textbf{Our Research Focus Areas:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=15pt, itemsep=2pt, topsep=4pt]
    \item Agentic AI \& Large Language Models for Business Automation
    \item Robotics, UAVs \& Autonomous Ground/Aerial Systems
    \item Medical \& Health Robotics (Surgery, Rehabilitation, Medical Imaging)
    \item Cloud Robotics \& Multi-Robot Systems
\end{itemize}
\end{aboutbox}

% About the Position
\section{About the Position}

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated \textbf{Research Engineer} to join our dynamic robotics team at AlfaisalX. This is an exceptional opportunity for a passionate roboticist to work on cutting-edge projects that will shape the future of autonomous systems in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

The ideal candidate will have proven hands-on experience with ROS/ROS 2, Gazebo simulation, and integration of robotic systems including UAVs, ground robots, and mobile platforms. You will work alongside world-class researchers, collaborate with industry partners such as \textbf{HUMAIN}, \textbf{Bako Motors}, and \textbf{Tawuniya}, and contribute to projects that have real-world impact.

As a Research Engineer, you will be at the forefront of developing next-generation robotic solutions, from autonomous navigation systems to intelligent drone fleets. You will have access to state-of-the-art equipment including high-end UAV platforms, mobile robots, motion capture systems, and powerful GPU workstations for deep learning.

This role offers significant growth opportunities, including the possibility of pursuing a Ph.D. for exceptional candidates and co-authoring publications in top robotics venues such as ICRA, IROS, and RA-L.
